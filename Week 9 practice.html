Tacoma Public Schools are so great that there is a waitlist to enroll your child.

Many high profile individuals from around the country, and even the world, have signed up to enroll their children.

Click the buttons below to add your name to the waitlist, or view the current waitlist.

If you get impatient with waiting, we have added a new feature which allows you to remove others from the waitlist, improving your child's chances of attending.

You can access this feature by clicking the button that says "Remove from the Waitlist."
